I'm

web developer, CS educated, IT experienced

I'm a web developer who can do front-end and back-end work, with a Master's in Computer Science, and work experience as an Information Technology (IT) technician.

I first started off working in the IT departments of various companies, building my skills in troubleshooting a myriad of issues, both hardware and software. I also worked on networking problems and administered servers on various platforms, including Linux and Microsoft Windows Server. After a while, I decided I wanted a fresh challenge and took up computer programming at the University of California, Irvine, where I earned a Master's degree. But I didn't stop there. I wanted to specialize in web development, and that led me to a coding boot camp called Learning Fuze where I developed the required skills to create full-stack web applications.
